What is Dily?

AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAs Dily is a service that allows you to plot your life's timeline by remembering the important moments you experienced and the people you shared them with. Here you can easily add memoryes from your holidays or other important days in your life in order to share them to your friends. And because moments are better with photos, Dily momoryes are a great reason to bring your old photos into the digital world.

What is a memory?

A memory can be anything such as a favourite holiday with your best friends, a great party, a wedding or that amazing concert you went to.
